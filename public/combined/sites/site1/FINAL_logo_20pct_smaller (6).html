    Cases We Handle

    Truck Accidents

    Truck accident cases are complex, with unique laws, regulations, and technology most firms can’t handle. Our dedicated truck accident team handles more cases than any other firm in Michigan.

    Car Accidents

    We handle car accident cases every day—hundreds of thousands and counting. Our team makes insurance companies pay every dollar you deserve, securing Michigan’s largest verdicts and settlements.

    Motorcycle Accidents

    Motorcycle accidents involve unique legal challenges. You need a lawyer who knows Michigan motorcycle law to get full compensation. We’ve won one of Michigan’s largest motorcycle accident settlements.

    Uber & Lyft Accidents

    Rideshare accidents come with hidden challenges, from terms of service that limit your rights to key app data that can serve as evidence. We handle the complexities to get you the most money possible.

    Pedestrian Accidents

    Pedestrian accident cases are complex. Juror bias, disputed liability, and lack of evidence can make proving fault tough. Our team navigates these challenges to secure maximum compensation for victims.

    Dog Bite Injuries

    Dog bite injuries go beyond the physical—survivors often face lasting trauma. If you’ve been bitten, evidence is crucial. Our top-rated dog bite lawyers build your case to get you the most money possible.
